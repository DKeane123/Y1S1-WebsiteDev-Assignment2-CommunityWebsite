The Knockbeg Area

There is so much to do in Knockbeg, from visiting the local sports club to the beutiful river to the local castle ruins

The Community Hall and Sports Club

Our local community center is full of activities every day of the week. With games nights for the children and losts of sport related training clubs there is so much for young kids to get involved during their time in the area. There are also our monthly trips to wonderful parts of Ireland and GAA matchs which the details can be found on our events page available above

Our local river

The local river is full to the brim with daily tourism and water related activities. It provides a great place to relax in the sun with the family, to enjoy some kyaking and enjoy the beuty of nature or fish at one of the many piers along the riverbank

The Medival Castle

Our local medival ruins are a wonder to behold. These ruins used to be a castle and priory hundreds of years ago. Now with the help of our wonderful tour guides you can step back into history and learn about what medival society was like. There is also our gift shop so you can take a piece of the magic home
